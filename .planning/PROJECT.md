# CareerSignal

## What This Is

A professional job discovery platform for mid-career switchers, where community discussion is the filter. Instead of scrolling through hundreds of irrelevant listings, users find roles through shared intel, company commentary, and peer conversation — making every job feel vetted before you even apply.

## Core Value

Mid-career switchers land roles by finding opportunities the community has already talked about, reviewed, and validated — not by sending applications into a void.

### Active

- [ ] User can discover job listings enriched with community discussion and intel
- [ ] User can post and read commentary on specific roles and companies
- [ ] User can create a profile that reflects their career trajectory and target roles
- [ ] User can connect and follow other professionals in their target field
- [ ] User receives personalized job recommendations based on background and goals
- [ ] Employers/recruiters can post roles and engage with community feedback

### Out of Scope

- Direct messaging between strangers — focus on public community discussion first
- Course or skills curriculum — job discovery is the core, not education
- AI resume builder — defer, not the primary value prop

## Context

- Primary audience: mid-career professionals switching industries or leveling up (not new grads, not executives)
- The pain: job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed) are too noisy — listings feel generic, applications go unanswered
- The insight: community context (what's it actually like to work there? is this role legit?) is what mid-career switchers need but can't easily find
- Platform: undecided — web-first recommended to ship faster, with mobile to follow

## Constraints

- **Audience focus**: Mid-career switchers — not trying to serve everyone
- **Community-first**: Every product decision should amplify the community signal, not bypass it
